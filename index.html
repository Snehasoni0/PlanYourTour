Which Time is the best for visiting Kerala?

+

Winter Season October to March is the ideal time to visit Kerala. At this time you get the best experience of KeralaNature by exploring beautiful landscapes, lush greenery, and serene backwaters, and best for outdoor activities and sightseeing.

If you visit Kerala for Ayurvedic treatments, then June to September (monsoon) is the ideal time. For Budget travelers, the Summer months (March to May) is the best time.
